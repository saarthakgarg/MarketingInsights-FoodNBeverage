## Project Overview

This project analyzes consumer behavior, brand perception, and marketing effectiveness in the Food & Beverage (F&B) industry, using survey data from an energy drink brand (CodeX).
The objective is to generate actionable marketing and revenue growth insights that can help a brand improve awareness, trial, and purchase conversion.

## Problem statement

CodeX is a German beverage company that is aiming to make its mark in the Indian market. A few months ago, they launched their energy drink in 10 cities in India.

Their Marketing team is responsible for increasing brand awareness, market share, and product development. They conducted a survey in those 10 cities and received results from 10k respondents. You are a marketing data analyst tasked to convert these survey results to meaningful insights which the team can use to drive actions.

### Task

- Check the “Primary_Secondary_Insights.pdf” and answer the questions by analyzing the data provided. 
- The target audience is the Chief Marketing Officer, you have 15 minutes to present insights to him. Make a concise presentation. You can add more research questions and answer them in your presentation that suits your recommendations. You can use additional data based on your own research to support your recommendations
- Please note that it is possible that some respondents might not have answered all the questions sincerely. For instance, a person is more likely to have not tasted the drink if they haven’t heard about it. Sense-check the data and use your judgment to clean it as required.
